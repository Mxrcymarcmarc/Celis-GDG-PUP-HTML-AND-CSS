My Hobby is Cooking

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of Samyang
  • 2 cups of Milk/Evap
  • 1 cup of cheese of your choice (I choose parmesan and cheddar)
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps to Get Started:

  1. Step 1: Boil 2 cups of milk making sure the fire is medium so and to stir so that it won't burn.
  2. Step 2: Season it with salt and pepper
  3. Step 3: Drop your cheese in and wait for it to boil.
  4. Step 4: Once it boils, put the Samyang noodles in and wait for the milk to reduce to a desired consistency.
  5. Step 5: Once the noodle cooks, fold the Samyang sauce and sprinkle the seaweed packet and enjoy.
